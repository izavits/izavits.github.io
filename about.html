(Really) Short Bio

I was born in Athens, Greece, although my origin is from Meganissi island. I grew up and studied Informatics and Telecommunications at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. I got my PhD at the National Center of Scientific Research "Demokritos" and at the Department of Information & Communication Systems Enginnering - University of the Aegean under the supervision of Prof. George A. Vouros and Dr. Georgios Paliouras.

I am member of the PASCAL network of excellence and member of the Hellenic Artificial Intelligence Society.

My research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, Text Mining, Information Retrieval, Data Science, while my favorite programming languages are Javascript (in NodeJS environments) and Python.
